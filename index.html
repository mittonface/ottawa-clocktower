What is Blood on the Clocktower?

Blood on the Clocktower is the world's greatest social deduction game — think Werewolf or Mafia, but every player gets a unique and exciting role — and death isn't the end. A live Storyteller hosts each game, guiding the narrative and making sure everyone has a great time.

Learn more at The Pandemonium Institute's official website.

How We Play
🤝

In-Person Games

Currently we're meeting up once per month in Kanata to play Clocktower how it was intended to be played — around a circle with a group of friends. We're a friendly and inclusive group, and we usually run a beginner-friendly game alongside one for more experienced players.

Monthly
💻

Online Games

Between our in-person games we run more frequent online games using the official app. This lets us try new scripts, practice lying, or just get your fix when you can't wait for the next in-person session.

Multiple times per month
